Edu-Drum Machine

Understand how 1s and 0s become a groove.

How it works

Click the Play button to start the clock. Each column represents a "step" in time. When the highlight hits a colored square, the computer executes a snippet of code to generate sound!

The Kick Drum: Synthesized by a Sine wave that rapidly drops in pitch from 150Hz to 0.01Hz. This "thump" mimics the physical hit of a mallet on a large drum skin.
The Snare Drum: Uses "White Noise" (random frequencies) combined with a quick oscillating burst. It mimics the rattling metal wires (snares) on the bottom of a real drum.
The Hi-Hat: Created using a High-Pass Filter on white noise. It cuts out all the low "heavy" frequencies, leaving only the sharp "ticking" sound.
